Data Deletion

Raku JLPT Vocabulary by CleanDino

This page explains how to request deletion of your data in Raku JLPT Vocabulary.

Delete your data in the app

If you have the app installed, you can delete all of your community data instantly:

  1. Open the app and go to the Community tab.
  2. Tap the Settings (gear) icon at the top right.
  3. Choose Leave / Delete community data.
  4. Confirm. Your data is permanently deleted immediately.

What gets deleted

The following data is permanently deleted and is not retained:

  • Your randomly generated nickname and profile emoji
  • Your learning statistics used for rankings (daily/weekly/streak)
  • Your follow relationships
  • Your push notification token
  • Your anonymous account

Note on anonymous accounts

This app uses anonymous accounts only — we never collect your name, email, phone number, or password. Because accounts are anonymous, the only way to identify and delete your specific data is from within the app while you are signed in. If you have already uninstalled the app, no personally identifiable information remains linked to you on our servers.
